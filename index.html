📖 How to Get Started
1
Click ▶ START CAPTURE above
2
Select the Entire Screen tab
3
Choose which screen you'd like to share
4
Toggle on "Also share system audio" at the bottom
5
Click Share — and enjoy the visuals! 🎶

⚠️ "Share system audio" is required — without it, the visualizer won't respond to music.

🌐 Works in Chrome, Edge, Brave, Arc, and Opera only. Not supported on Safari or mobile browsers.
